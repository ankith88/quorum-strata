How the tender engine works
Each compliance item is assigned a category (pest, fire, lift, cleaning, electrical, plumbing, insurance broker). Each category has a curated supplier panel — the strata manager's approved network. When the system detects an upcoming due date, it auto-issues a tender to 2–3 panel suppliers with standardised scope. Suppliers respond electronically. Committee receives one notification. Quotes display side-by-side with comparable line items. Committee approves with one tap. Supplier locked, scheduled, audit trail recorded.
