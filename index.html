The thesis

Committees rotate every 2–3 years. Buildings persist for decades. Today, that institutional memory leaves with each outgoing chair's email archive — and committees, managers, and owners all pay for it. Quorum is the structured workspace that retains it, surfaces it for the next chair, and turns the day-to-day chase into one-tap decisions. Trust accounting stays where it is.

What Quorum does

Three product primitives, one workflow.

01 / Intake

Email-native, magic-link access

Every email to your scheme alias becomes a structured ticket. Replies to the thread automatically. Committee members never manage a password — every action begins with a notification email and one tap into the relevant decision.

02 / Compliance

Auto-tender to your approved panel

Pest. Fire. Lift. Cleaning. Insurance. Each recurring obligation auto-issues a scope to quote 60 days before due, returns comparable quotes side-by-side, and locks in with a single tap. The 11-day chase becomes a one-tap vote.

03 / Memory

Audit-grade decision log

Motion text, vote tally, attachments, time-stamped. Tamper-evident 7-year retention. Auto-archive on committee rollover. Section 184 certificates near-automatic. The next chair starts day one with full institutional context.
